Skill definition

What a skill means in AgentHub

A skill is a reusable capability package that gives an agent a concrete job function, such as browsing the web, reading documents, coordinating schedules, or validating compliance logic. Admin users manage the catalog to understand what behaviors are available and how heavily each capability is used across the customer base.
